## Chat with your local model

Open the **LLMStack** view in the activity bar (left edge) and ask anything.

- Pick a model from the dropdown at the top.
- Toggle **Include editor context** to send your current selection or file.
- Every code block in a reply has **Apply**, **Insert**, and **Copy** buttons.
- Rate replies with 👍 / 👎 — feedback trains your local model over time.

Everything runs against your gateway. Nothing is uploaded.
